About CDF

Your favourite (if not, soon-to-be) furniture store. Founded by our personal exasperation in sourcing for quality and beautiful pieces at reasonable prices in Singapore.

CDF is proud to call ourselves a homegrown brand. We believe that high-quality furniture should come at an affordable price. Driven by a high standard of service, we provide stellar customer care even after your purchase.

We do not have a showroom and we operate 100% online.
